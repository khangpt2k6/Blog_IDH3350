How to use this diagram

Click any element to learn more

This diagram maps the Kollmuss & Agyeman (2002) model of pro-environmental behavior. The central flow shows the path from Knowledge → Awareness → Attitude → Behavior. The ⚡🔥🚧 icons represent barriers that break this chain. Chips above and below show external and internal factors that shape or block behavior.

Start by clicking the red "THE GREEN GAP" barrier — it's the core finding of the paper.

Flow node — step in the chain
Barrier — friction between steps
External factor — outside the individual
Internal factor — inside the individual
The Green Gap — biggest barrier
Source: Kollmuss, A., & Agyeman, J. (2002). Mind the gap: Why do people act environmentally and what are the barriers to pro-environmental behavior? Environmental Education Research, 8(3), 239–260. https://doi.org/10.1080/13504620220145401
