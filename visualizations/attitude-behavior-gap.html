How to read this

A quick guide to the model

This diagram maps the chain from Knowledge → Awareness → Attitude → Behavior, based on Kollmuss & Agyeman (2002). Each circle between nodes highlights where the chain often breaks (a barrier). The factor chips above and below show external and internal forces that can widen or narrow those gaps.

Source: Kollmuss, A., & Agyeman, J. (2002). Mind the gap: Why do people act environmentally and what are the barriers to pro-environmental behavior? Environmental Education Research, 8(3), 239–260. doi:10.1080/13504620220145401  ·  Visualization for USF Student Research Blog · April 2026
